ABOUT US

At ML, we are five friends united by a common passion: matcha. More than just a coffee shop, we are a place where we celebrate the connection between natural, healthy and delicious. Our mission is to share with you not only our obsession with matcha of the highest quality, but also the warmth and the warmth and creativity we put into every cup.

FAQs about Matcha Tea

What is matcha tea?

+

Matcha tea is a type of powdered green tea made from high-quality leaves.

What are its benefits?

+

Matcha tea enhances concentration, boosts metabolism, and is rich in antioxidants.

How is matcha tea prepared?

+

The powder is mixed with hot water and whisked until a frothy texture is achieved.

Where is the best matcha tea grown?

+

The best matcha comes from Japan, especially from Uji and Nishio.

Does matcha tea contain caffeine?

+

Yes, but in smaller amounts than coffee and it provides sustained energy.

Is matcha suitable for vegans?

+

Yes, matcha is completely vegan and natural.

How long does matcha tea last when stored?

+

Properly stored, matcha tea remains fresh for up to 6 months.

What utensils do I need to prepare it?

+

A chasen (bamboo whisk) and a bowl are ideal for its preparation.

Can I use milk instead of water?

+

Yes, you can make a delicious matcha latte using plant-based or animal milk.

How many cups of matcha can I drink per day?

+

It is recommended to drink 1-2 cups per day to enjoy its benefits.
